Sivananda Classical Hatha Yoga is a traditional practice based on the Five Points of Yoga including proper exercise, proper breathing, proper relaxation, proper diet, and meditation/positive thinking.

The physical practice combines meditation, breath work, and asanas to provide healing to the body, mind, and spirit.

The atmosphere is quiet and relaxing, with practitioners focused on their inward state.

Each class includes sanskrit chanting, breath work, asanas (postures), and a final relaxation.

Classes are suitable for any level of practitioner.

What is iRest?

Please join us for a unique and grounding experience with this guided meditation and relaxation practice based on the teachings of psychologist and yogi, Richard Miller, PhD.

Grounded in the ancient teachings of meditation, iRest is an evidence-based transformative practice that leads to psychological, physical, and spiritual healing and well-being.

iRest provides you with tools to help you relax deeply, release stress, increase resiliency, improve your interpersonal relationships and provide you with greater mastery and control in your life.

Participants often report:

  • Reduced depression, fear and anxiety 
  • Reduced insomnia and levels of stress 
  • Reduced chronic and acute pain 
  • Greater ability to relax and enjoy life 
  • Improved interpersonal relations 
  • Increased inner peace and well-being
